Microwave Jacket Potato

You can cook a jacket potato in the microwave.

Method

Scrub a potato clean. Microwave for 2-5 minutes, depending on size. Serve with a generous amount of butter.

Notes

Do not peel the potato, it will come out funny. If the potato isn't cooked after 5 minutes, it is probably too big to cook well. If the butter is salted, you shouldn't need any extra. Original recipe
