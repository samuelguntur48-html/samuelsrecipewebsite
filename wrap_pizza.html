Wrap Pizza

This a vague guidleine for making a simple pizza using a wrap as a base.

Ingredients

  • Wrap
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Grated Cheese
  • Other toppings

Method

Preheat your oven to 180-200° C. Put a wrap on a baking tray and spread with sauce. Add the other toppings and cover with cheese. Cook for 5-10 minutes.
