Pancakes

These pancakes are thick and fluffy. Serves 4.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups Milk

Method

Mix the dry ingredients together. Then mix in the wet ingredients. Whisk until smooth. Fry.

Notes

I have doubled the baking powder and removed the butter. You could also seive the flour and baking powder.
