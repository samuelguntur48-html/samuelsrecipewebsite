Original Introduction

This is a recipe book.
"Recipe" is pronounced rek - ip - ee. Or wreck - a - pea. They both work. This is Samuel K. Guntur's collection of important recipes. He did not feel that it was important to write down unimportant recipes. Reasons: This would be a collection of rewritten recipes books, some of the recipes would be weird and/or have too many vegetables and also Samuel cannot be bothered to do that much writing. I, Samuel K. Guntur, have made all these dishes before and can confirm that they taste very good.
