Fudge

This is an excellent recipe for delicious, golden, creamy microwave fudge. Makes a small-medium batch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Golden Syrup
  • ~400g Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 100g Butter
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence

Method

Mix the Condensed Milk, Suigar, Butter and Golden Syrup together in a suitable bowl. It will need to be large enough for the fudge to bubble up. Then, begin microwaving the fudge in 2 minute increments, watching constantly. Do this up to 5-6 times. When the fudge is boiling all over, see if it has reached 115 degrees. Keep measuring with a thermometer. The fudge should turn smooth and a deep gold colour. Once it reaches 115 degrees, add the vanilla essence and beat the fudge for about 5 minutes. It will get much thicker as it cools. Then, pour it into a tin lined with baking paper and let it set. Then you can cut it.
