Weetbix Slice

This is a chocolate slice made with Weetbix topped with chocolate icing.

Ingredients

  • ~4 Crushed Weetbix
  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 3 Tbsp Cocoa Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 150 g Melted Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Essence

  • 1 1/2 cups Icing Sugar (225 g)
  • 3 Tbsp Cocoa Powder
  • 25 g Butter
  • 2 Tbsp Boiling Water

Method

Preheat oven to 180° fan forced. Mix together the dry ingredients. Then make a well in the center add the wet ingredients. Press into tin. Bake for 10 minutes, until cooked but still soft to touch in center. While it is cooking, mix the butter, sugar and cocoa powder. Then add enough boiling water to melt the butter and make a very thick icing. Ice while hot. Cut when cooler.
